What is this app about?
The primary purpose of this app is to help people plan, manage and collaborate on potlucks easier and more efficiently.
Can I use this app for other events like baby showers, birthdays and other events?
While this app was originally intended for potlucks, you can use this app for events as you see fit.
What are some of the key features about this app?
  • Create your own custom potluck lists (e.g. Appetizers, Board games, Snacks, Movies, etc).
  • Easily RSVP with potluck items.
  • Easily track the total amount of guests attending, not coming and undecided.
  • An easy-to-use dashboard which provides the information you need fast
  • Easily notify groups of individuals or individual guests at a time.
  • Automated email reminders that remind guests to rsvp and that the event is coming.

Great! What do I need to do in order to start using this?
First, create an account.
What is the bare minimum that I need to provide in order to create an event?
After you create an account, you should be able to create events pretty easily. At a bare minimum, you just need to provide a name for the event, the date of the event, and when you need guests to rsvp by.
How do I invite guests?
As a host, you'll be able to invite guest using the Invite guest option when you are viewing the details of the event. You'll be able to invite guests via comma-delimited emails (e.g. email1, email2, email3). This is the preferred method of adding the guests to your event since the system will automatically take care of adding them rather than you manually providing that information via The guest list.
Can I manually add guests without using the Invite guest option?
I understand that certain guests may not have email and would rather call you instead of using the system. For this purpose, this app will allow you to rsvp on their behalf. You can do so by using the The guest list option (enter guest manually and rsvp for them).
Any other advice for getting started?
I would recommend creating your potluck list before inviting guests. While it is possible for your guests to rsvp without declaring what they are bringing, it might be good for them to get an idea of what is needed for the event.
What kind of lists can I create?
You can pretty much create any type of list you wish: Appetizers, Snacks, Beer/Alcohol, Boardgames, Movies, Xbox games, etc. For example, create a list with Games as your category and enter each game on a separate field (you can dynamically add as many items as you wish for a particular category).
Do you have any recommendations on effectively creating list?
You can pretty much create any type of list you wish: Appetizers, Snacks, Beer/Alcohol, Boardgames, Movies, Xbox games, etc. However, it's best that you be as specific as possible with your categories so that your items will be easily categorized and specific. A category of Food and having 30+ items is not an easy way to provide lists for your guests AND does not provide you an easy way to see what guests are bringing. Therefore, having specific categories like Snacks, you can then differentiate items like Chips/Salsa, Pretzels, Peanuts, Bite-size candy. The next list could then be Appetizers with items such as Spring Rolls, Nachos, Taquitos, Buffalo Wings.
What is this Host quantity that I am seeing when I create my list?
That is a poorly-labeled field. That is meant to represent the number of items you need for guest to contribute for the potluck. For example, you create a list of category Boardgames with a host quantity of 2 and provide 5 games as suggestions. This is primarily for you to keep track of whether or not your "quota" for that particular category has been met.
When I send someone a message, how does that work?
The message gets routed to their declared email account. Therefore, they will get the message as you send it.
Why can I message some people and others I cannot?
Thats because the host added them to the system without specifying what their email address is. You are only allowed to message guests that have email addresses declared.

I want to RSVP for an event I was invited to. How difficult is it?
It should be pretty easy. Once you sign in, select the event you wish to rsvp to. Click "Modify RSVP" to enter your response.
What is the bare minimum I need to RSVP?
By default, your response is Undecided. You only need to change that answer. However, once you decide you are going, you will be presented another list of options. Those options include:
  • # of guests you are bringing (excluding you)
  • Any items you wish to bring
You also have the option of providing a comment (or updating your comment to let the host know of any updates).
Do I have to RSVP with an item?
No. You may decide what to bring to the event later but inform the host you plan on coming.
What if I want to bring more than one item (or if I am answering on behalf of a guest I am bringing)?
If you are bringing someone to an event you are invited to (such as a friend and/or partner), you may add items to your rsvp and just provide a short comment saying that you and your guests are bringing those items. This app allows for multiple items to be umbrellaed under one RSVP response (e.g. You could be bringing a guest and each of you brings an item. Therefore, you can add another item to your rsvp (Just press the Add more button).
